Info about group:
Kis-Kis is a Russian rock band founded in November 2018 in St. Petersburg. The ensemble consists of four members - drummer Alina Olesheva, vocalist Sofia Somuseva, guitarist Yuri Zaslonov and bassist Sergei Ivanov.
The band gained fame thanks to the album "Youth in punk style" and the first two singles "lbtd" and "shut up". In almost two years of its existence, a total of 10 video clips were shot. The clip "keep quiet" gained viral popularity, due to which the collective for the first year of its existence performed on "Invasion" and appeared in the program "Evening Urgant".
